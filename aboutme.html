in Sohee



Sohee means bright wish.
but when she went to the exhibition of Jaime Hayon, she saw a phrase - I believe that design should provoke emotions. design should make you feel good. create happiness. - and was deeply impressed. Because it was the answer to the vague question of why you are designing, which you have not always thought deeply. Then she discovered that her name could have another identity: a little joy, not a bright wish. So she wants to be a designer who creates a little bit of fun.
